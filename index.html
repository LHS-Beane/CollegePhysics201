Course Overview

This 8-week course covers core topics in calculus-based mechanics: kinematics, forces, work and energy, momentum, rotational dynamics, and oscillations. Each week has complete notes, worked examples, practice problems, and simulations built for independent learning.

Mechanics Foundations

Vectors, 1D & 2D motion, Newton’s Laws, and applying derivatives and integrals to describe motion.

Energy & Momentum

Work–energy methods, potential energy, conservation of energy and momentum, and real collision models.

Rotation & Oscillations

Torque, angular momentum, rolling motion, and simple harmonic motion with calculus-based descriptions.
